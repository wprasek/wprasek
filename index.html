How it works

We reduce double handling, improve transparency and ultimately save everyone a lot of time and hassle.

Tenant
Nick submits a lease application

including consent for contact from Connect Express, which we automatically detect. Nice!

Tenant
Nick receives an SMS / email from CX

and selects a great value energy plan in a few quick taps, as most of his info is pre-filled. Done!

Agent
Sarah tracks her payments in realtime

including alerts for any rejected connections and weekly or monthly reports. Winning!
